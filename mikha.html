Mikhaela Janna Jimenea Lim (born November 8, 2003) better known as Mikha, is a Filipino artist and performer.
She is the third youngest member, main rapper, lead dancer and visual of the Filipino Girl Group BINI.
Mikha's birth name is Mikhaela Janna Lim. She was born in Cebu, then they lived in Sta. Rosa, Laguna for 2 years, moved to Silang, Cavite for 6 years.
Her family, her father, her mother, and her two siblings currently live in San Juan City, Metro Manila.
